Before You Contact Us

Please read the following carefully to ensure your query reaches the right place:

❌ We CANNOT Help With:

  • Order issues or refunds
  • Product complaints
  • Store-specific questions
  • Employment enquiries
  • Gift card issues
  • Delivery problems
  • Allergen queries for ordering
  • Stock availability

For these issues, contact Greggs directly.

✅ We CAN Help With:

  • Website errors or bugs
  • Incorrect menu information
  • Suggestions for improvements
  • General feedback about this site
  • Partnership enquiries
  • Copyright concerns
  • Privacy questions
  • Technical issues with the site

What to Include in Your Message

To help us respond efficiently, please include:

  • Clear subject line: Briefly describe your enquiry
  • Specific details: Which page or information you're referring to
  • Screenshots: If reporting an error, screenshots are helpful
  • Your suggestion: If suggesting improvements, be as detailed as possible

Response Time

We aim to respond to all genuine enquiries within 5-7 working days. Please note that this is a small, independently-run website, so response times may vary. We appreciate your patience.

Reporting Errors

Spotted incorrect information on our site? We appreciate you letting us know! Please include:

  • The specific page URL where the error appears
  • What information is incorrect
  • What the correct information should be (with source if possible)
  • The date you noticed the error

We strive for accuracy but recognise that menu items and prices change frequently. Your corrections help keep the site useful for everyone.

Suggestions and Feedback

  • Improvements to navigation or design

Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't you have a phone number?

As a small, independently-run website, we manage enquiries via email to ensure we can respond thoughtfully and keep records of correspondence.

Do you have social media accounts?

Currently, Greggs-Menu.net does not maintain social media accounts. All official communication is through this website and the email address provided above.

Can you put me in touch with Greggs?

We have no special relationship with Greggs plc. Please use the official Greggs contact methods listed on this page.

I sent an email but haven't received a response

Please allow 5-7 working days for a response. If your query was about Greggs products or services rather than this website, we may not be able to respond as we only handle website-related enquiries.

Can you add my business/link to your website?

We generally do not accept unsolicited link or advertising requests. If you have a genuine partnership proposal, please email with full details.

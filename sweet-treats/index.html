The Story of Greggs Sweet Treats

Bakery Heritage

Greggs started as a bakery in 1939, and sweet treats have been at the heart of the business ever since. The recipes used today are based on decades of baking expertise, with many items made using traditional methods passed down through generations of bakers.

Fresh Daily

Unlike many chain retailers, Greggs bakes fresh products in-store throughout the day. Doughnuts, pastries, and cakes are made in batches to ensure maximum freshness, with some shops baking multiple times daily to keep up with demand.

Unbeatable Value

Greggs sweet treats offer exceptional value compared to competitors. Where coffee shops might charge ¬£3+ for a cake or cookie, Greggs prices start from just ¬£0.75, making quality bakery treats accessible to everyone.

Sweet Treats FAQs

Are Greggs doughnuts made fresh?

Yes! Greggs doughnuts and pastries are baked fresh in-store daily. They're not delivered pre-made ‚Äì each shop has bakers who prepare sweet treats throughout the day to ensure freshness.

What time does Greggs put out fresh doughnuts?

This varies by shop, but most Greggs locations have fresh doughnuts available from opening time, with additional batches made throughout the day. Peak baking times are typically early morning and lunchtime.

Can you freeze Greggs sweet treats?

Whilst technically possible, freezing isn't recommended as it affects texture and quality. Greggs sweet treats are best enjoyed fresh on the day of purchase. However, most items will keep for 1-2 days at room temperature in an airtight container.

Why are Greggs sweet treats so cheap?

Greggs keeps prices low through efficient operations, bulk ingredient purchasing, and high-volume sales. By baking in-store and operating on slim margins, they can offer quality bakery items at prices significantly lower than competitors.

Are there any vegan sweet treats?

Yes! Greggs offers several vegan doughnuts and sweet options. Check the current menu as offerings change seasonally. Staff can advise on current vegan options.

Do sweet treats contain allergens?

Yes, most sweet treats contain gluten (wheat), eggs, and milk. Some also contain nuts. Always check the allergen information in-store or online if you have allergies or dietary requirements.

Can I order large quantities for parties?

Many Greggs shops can accommodate bulk orders for parties and events. It's best to call ahead (ideally a day in advance) to discuss quantities and collection times with your local shop.

Why Choose Greggs for Breakfast?

Greggs has become one of the UK's favourite breakfast destinations, and for good reason. Here's what makes a Greggs breakfast special:

Freshness Guaranteed

All breakfast items are prepared fresh each morning in-store. The bacon is cooked to perfection, eggs are free-range, and bread is baked daily. You can taste the difference that fresh ingredients make.

Value for Money

Greggs breakfast menu offers exceptional value without compromising on quality. With prices starting from just ¬£1.65 for porridge and hearty options under ¬£4, you can enjoy a satisfying breakfast without breaking the bank.

Quick Service

Perfect for busy mornings, Greggs offers quick service whether you're grabbing breakfast on the go or sitting in. Most items are ready to go, making it ideal for commuters and early risers.

Variety of Options

From traditional bacon rolls to vegan alternatives, healthy porridge to indulgent breakfast baguettes, there's something for every preference and dietary requirement.

Breakfast Menu FAQs

What time does Greggs serve breakfast?

Greggs breakfast menu is typically available from opening time (usually between 6:30am and 7:30am) until 11:00am. Some items may be available after 11am subject to availability.

Can I get a Greggs breakfast all day?

The dedicated breakfast menu is available until 11am. However, some breakfast items like bacon rolls and sausage rolls may be available throughout the day at selected shops.

Does Greggs do breakfast meal deals?

Yes! Greggs offers breakfast meal deals that combine a breakfast item with a hot drink for great value. Prices and availability may vary by location.

Are there vegetarian breakfast options at Greggs?

Yes, Greggs offers several vegetarian breakfast options including egg and cheese baguettes, beans and cheese toasties, and porridge.

What vegan breakfast options does Greggs have?

Greggs offers vegan sausage breakfast rolls and porridge (when made with oat or soya milk). Check with your local shop for the full range of vegan breakfast options.

How many calories are in a Greggs bacon roll?

A Greggs bacon roll contains approximately 325 calories. For detailed nutritional information on all breakfast items, visit our nutrition page.

Can I order Greggs breakfast for delivery?

Yes, Greggs breakfast can be ordered for delivery through Just Eat and Uber Eats in selected locations. Check the apps for availability in your area.

The Best Greggs Breakfast Items

Here are the most popular breakfast items according to Greggs fans across the UK:

1. Bacon Roll

The undisputed champion of the Greggs breakfast menu. Three rashers of crispy bacon in a soft white roll - simple, satisfying, and perfectly executed every time. Add brown sauce for the ultimate breakfast experience.

2. Sausage, Bacon & Egg Wrap

For those who want it all, this hearty wrap combines sausage, bacon, and egg in one convenient package. It's filling, flavourful, and perfect for when you need a substantial breakfast.

3. Breakfast Flatbread

A newer addition that's quickly become a favourite. The combination of sausage, bacon, egg, and hash brown in a toasted flatbread offers excellent value and incredible taste.

4. Free Range Egg & Cheese Baguette

The best vegetarian option on the menu. The combination of free-range egg and mature cheddar in a freshly baked baguette is both wholesome and delicious.

5. Porridge

For health-conscious breakfast goers, Greggs porridge is a warm, comforting option made with jumbo oats. Add honey or fruit for extra flavour.

About Greggs

Greggs History

Founded in 1939 by John Gregg, Greggs has grown from a single bakery in Newcastle upon Tyne to over 2,300 shops across the UK. The family-run business became a national treasure, known for quality baked goods at affordable prices.

What Makes Greggs Special

Greggs is famous for its fresh, daily-baked products, iconic sausage rolls, and commitment to value. With both traditional favourites and modern vegan options, Greggs caters to all tastes whilst maintaining its British heritage.

Menu Variety

From breakfast items to lunchtime sandwiches, savoury bakes to sweet treats, and hot drinks to cold refreshments, Greggs offers a comprehensive menu perfect for any time of day.

Why Choose Greggs?

Greggs has become a staple of British high streets for good reason:

  • Affordability: Quality food at competitive prices
  • Consistency: The same great taste across all locations
  • Variety: Something for everyone, including vegan options
  • Convenience: Over 2,300 shops nationwide
  • Quality: Fresh products baked throughout the day
  • British Heritage: A trusted name since 1939

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Greggs' opening hours?

Greggs shops typically open between 6:30am and 7:30am and close between 5:00pm and 8:00pm, depending on location. Some shops in train stations and airports have extended hours.

Does Greggs serve hot food all day?

Yes, most Greggs locations serve hot food throughout the day, though breakfast items are usually available until 11am. Popular items like sausage rolls and bakes are available all day.

Are there vegan options at Greggs?

Yes! Greggs offers a growing range of vegan products, including the popular vegan sausage roll, vegan steak bake, and various vegan sweet treats. Check our vegan menu page for the complete list.

Can I order Greggs online?

Yes, many Greggs shops offer click and collect through the Greggs app, and delivery is available through Just Eat and Uber Eats in selected locations.

Does Greggs cater for parties and events?

Yes, Greggs offers catering platters perfect for meetings, parties, and events. You can order online or in-store.

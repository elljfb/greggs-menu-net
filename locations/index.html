Where You'll Find Greggs

Greggs shops are strategically located for maximum convenience across various settings:

High Streets and Town Centres

Most town and city centres have at least one Greggs, typically in prominent high street locations. These shops usually offer the full menu and have seating areas.

Shopping Centres

Major shopping centres and retail parks often have Greggs shops, sometimes with extended opening hours to match mall hours.

Transport Hubs

You'll find Greggs at:

  • Train Stations: Major railway stations across the UK have Greggs
  • Airports: Many UK airports have Greggs in departure lounges
  • Motorway Services: Service stations on major motorways often include Greggs
  • Bus Stations: Larger bus stations may have Greggs shops nearby

Note: Prices at airport, station, and motorway service locations may be higher than standard high street shops.

Business Districts

Office areas and business districts often have Greggs shops catering to the lunchtime crowd, sometimes with reduced weekend hours.

Retail Parks

Out-of-town retail parks frequently have Greggs locations with parking facilities and drive-through options at some sites.

Hospitals and Universities

Many large hospitals and university campuses have Greggs shops to serve staff, students, and visitors.

Greggs Across the UK

Greggs operates throughout the United Kingdom with particularly strong presence in certain regions:

North East England

As Greggs was founded in Newcastle upon Tyne, the North East has the highest concentration of shops per capita. Nearly every town has at least one Greggs, with multiple locations in cities like Newcastle, Durham, and Sunderland.

Scotland

Greggs has extensive coverage across Scotland, from Edinburgh and Glasgow to smaller towns in the Highlands. Scottish shops may occasionally have region-specific items.

Northern England

Yorkshire, Lancashire, and the North West have excellent Greggs coverage. Cities like Manchester, Leeds, Liverpool, and Sheffield have dozens of locations.

Midlands

Birmingham, Nottingham, Leicester, and surrounding areas are well-served with Greggs shops in most towns and cities.

Wales

Cardiff, Swansea, and other Welsh towns have Greggs locations, though coverage is less dense than in England.

Southern England

London has numerous Greggs locations, particularly in Zone 1 and transport hubs. Other southern cities and towns also have good coverage, though historically less dense than the North.

South West England

Bristol, Plymouth, and other South West cities have Greggs shops, with expansion continuing in this region.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many Greggs shops are there in the UK?

As of 2026, there are over 2,000 Greggs locations across the United Kingdom, with new shops opening regularly.

Can I order delivery from Greggs?

Yes! Many Greggs shops are available on food delivery platforms like Just Eat, Uber Eats, and Deliveroo. Check your preferred delivery app for availability in your area.

Do all Greggs shops have the same menu?

Most shops carry the core menu items, but availability can vary. Smaller locations may have a more limited range, and some regional variations exist.

Can I reserve items at my local Greggs?

Use the Click & Collect service via the Greggs app to order ahead and ensure your items are available for pickup.

Are there Greggs shops outside the UK?

Greggs primarily operates in the UK, with no permanent international locations. The company has occasionally trialled shops abroad but maintains its focus on the British market.

Why are some Greggs more expensive?

Shops in airports, train stations, and motorway services typically charge higher prices due to increased operating costs at these locations.

Sandwich FAQs

Are Greggs sandwiches made fresh?

Yes! Greggs sandwiches and baguettes are prepared fresh daily in each shop. They're made using quality ingredients and follow strict freshness guidelines to ensure the best quality.

Can I customise my sandwich?

Greggs sandwiches come pre-made with set ingredients to ensure consistency and quick service. However, staff may be able to accommodate simple requests like removing certain ingredients.

Do sandwich prices include VAT?

Cold sandwiches and baguettes are zero-rated for VAT, so the price you see is what you pay. Hot items like toasties are subject to VAT, which is already included in the displayed price.

What's the difference between a roll and a baguette?

Rolls are smaller and rounder, whilst baguettes are longer sub-style breads with more filling. Baguettes typically cost more (¬£3.50-¬£3.70) compared to rolls (around ¬£3.40) but offer larger portions.

Are any sandwiches suitable for vegetarians?

Yes! The Pesto and Mozzarella Pasta (¬£3.45) and Mature Cheddar Cheese Ploughmans Oval Bite (¬£3.60) are both vegetarian options. Always check current menu for the most up-to-date vegetarian choices.

How long do Greggs sandwiches last?

Greggs sandwiches have a same-day use-by date for maximum freshness. They should be consumed on the day of purchase and stored in a refrigerator if not eaten immediately.

Do Greggs sandwiches contain allergens?

Yes, various allergens are present including gluten, eggs, fish, milk, mustard, and soya. Always check the allergen information in-store or on the Greggs website before purchasing if you have allergies.
